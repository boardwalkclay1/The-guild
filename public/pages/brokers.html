Brokers & Banking Setup

To beat the arena, you will need a banker and a broker. And you can have more than one.

If you don’t already have a bank account, this is your first step. You will connect your bank account to your broker so you can move money in and out.

This is a small step and it is much easier than it feels. Do not get intimidated — you’ve signed up for apps before. This is the same thing, just more powerful.
