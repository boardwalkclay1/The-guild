Chart Patterns

Chart patterns are the language of the market. Once you learn to read them, you stop guessing and start recognizing.

A rounding bottom, a double bottom, a double top, a flag, a trend break — these are not just shapes. They are stories about what buyers and sellers are doing.

Knowing these patterns gives you a huge advantage. You will be able to see when a move is building, when it is failing, and when it is breaking out of its trend.
