The Bridge Between Your Money and the Market

Your bank holds your cash. Your broker gives you buying power. Connecting them unlocks your ability to trade.

Some brokers offer instant deposits. Others rely on slower ACH transfers.

Many will ask if you want a margin account, which allows borrowing but adds risk.
