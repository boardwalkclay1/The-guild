The Golden Rules

These are the rules you follow inside the market. These rules remove fear. These rules create confidence. These rules produce good trades.

1. Study the Night Before
The market is constantly moving. When you study the night before, you will see setups forming that should take place the next day. The market will always do what the market does — your job is to be prepared.
2. Stick to Your Game Plan
Come up with a plan from your studying the night before — and stick to it. Don’t be indecisive. Planning is what removes hesitation.
3. Be Patient
The market will move fast and try to make you jump in or get out prematurely. Be patient. Your moment will come — and patience is what makes it clear.
4. Setups Come With Even Numbers
Watch for even numbers. They cause big reactions — and big reactions create opportunity. Even numbers are where setups reveal themselves.
5. In The Money
You want your contracts in the money. If they aren’t — or won’t be soon — don’t take them. In‑the‑money contracts move cleaner, faster, and more predictably.
6. Quantity & Quality
More contracts = more money. But quality matters. The tradeoff is risk — and you must balance both. Reference: Death Strike
7. Never Marry a Trade
A part of your process is exiting the trade. Get out. You can always reposition the same way you got in. Never stay in a trade out of emotion.
