Moving Averages

Moving averages are the cleanest way to see the true direction of the market. They smooth out the noise and reveal the underlying path price has been traveling.

When a shorter moving average crosses above a longer one, it forms the Golden Cross — a powerful sign of strength and continuation. When it crosses below, you get the Death Cross, a warning of weakness.

You will learn to watch how these lines move, how they cross, and how they guide your entries and exits. They are not magic — they are structure. And structure is what removes fear and brings clarity.
