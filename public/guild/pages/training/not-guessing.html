Not Guessing, Not Gambling

XP: 0

This is the first Golden Rule for a reason. If you are guessing, you are gambling. If you are gambling, you are not trading — you are donating.

Every trade must be built on logic, structure, and confirmation. You must know why you are entering, where you are entering, and what you are looking for before you ever click the button.

Never guess. Never gamble. If you cannot explain your trade in clear, simple logic, you have no business taking it.

The Guild is a community of disciplined traders. We do not chase. We do not hope. We execute.

This rule is still under construction. More examples and deeper logic will be added.
